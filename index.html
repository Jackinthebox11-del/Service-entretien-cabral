Our Services

  • Janitorial Services – Daily cleaning tasks such as dusting, wiping surfaces, vacuuming, mopping and emptying trash keep workspaces clean and organized[1].
  • Restroom & Sanitization – Thorough cleaning and disinfection of restrooms helps prevent the spread of germs and maintains hygiene standards[2].
  • Floor Stripping & Waxing – Stripping away old finish and applying fresh wax revitalizes floors and provides a protective barrier against scratches and stains[3]. Waxed floors are safer, easier to clean, and extend the life of your flooring[4].
  • Window & Specialized Cleaning – Regular window cleaning improves light and ambiance[5], and we tailor specialized cleaning solutions for unique facilities[5].

About Us

Based in Montréal, Service Entretien Ménager Cabral specializes in janitorial cleaning and floor care for commercial and residential clients. Our professional team performs daily cleaning, restroom sanitization and detailed floor maintenance to keep your space clean and healthy[6]. We use eco‑friendly products and industry best practices to strip away worn finish, apply fresh wax and leave your floors shining like new[7]. By investing in professional floor care, you enjoy improved durability, enhanced safety and cost‑effective maintenance[8].
